◆ autosleep

unsigned autosleep

Activity and inactivity detection must be in linked mode or loop mode (LINK/LOOP bits in ACT_INACT_CTL register) to enable autosleep; otherwise, the bit is ignored. 1 = autosleep is enabled, and the device enters wake-up mode automatically upon detection of inactivity.
